FOOTBALL.

By Katt | August 15, 2010

This is why I am making an effort to improve my drawing:

The short story: It was time to work out, and I was procrastinating, so I drew this football guy.

I like him because he captures the look I was going for – kind of a looming brute strength – with only a few lines. Those doofy teeth are really supposed to be the helmet’s face mask, but I guess teeth work.

Although my goal is to produce good art, I’ve made a lot of bad art I find amusing.
