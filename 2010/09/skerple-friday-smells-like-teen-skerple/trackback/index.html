Skerple Friday: Smells like Teen Skerple

By Katt | September 17, 2010

Last night I had a dream that Kate Beaton, whose work I love*, had some sort of technical difficulties that prevented any dialogue or captions from appearing on her comics, so she held a caption contest on Twitter. I think all the comics were about babies.

Then I fell back asleep, and between Bonus Cat chewing tape and biting my scalp, I had dreams about washing underwear. And for some reason when I woke up I drew this.

I’m not completely sure why I have so much trouble drawing two eyes that look in the same direction.

There’s probably three dozen anachronisms lovingly hand-skerpled into the above, so I’m just gonna say (1) I was never very good at history (2) it’s, um, steampunk. She has cogs on her corset or something. You can get away with a lot if you say that it’s steampunk.

Of course, I have no reservations about mentioning my underwear or washing them in the machine.

“Stank” might actually be some sort of hot cereal, in which case Ke$ha? is going to get a surprise in thirty to forty-five minutes, or whenever she remembers that she put something in the washing machine.

*and who, I like to imagine, would TOTES be my BFF if we ever met. Same with Allie Brosh. I have a feeling, though, most 18-34 year old women think the same thing, and that if you are clever and talented and have a large fanbase, you probably have enough BFFs and wannabe BFFs for a while. No stalksies!
