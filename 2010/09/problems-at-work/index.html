Problems at work!

By Katt | September 9, 2010

Has it been a week? Dear goodness. I was sick, and then I was on vacation, and since I’ve gotten back I’ve been really tired. I was working on a few things before all that, which I need to finish up. In the meantime, we’ll always have Skerple Friday!

I found some old old stuff that I doodled in one of my work notebooks from a previous job. True stories, here!

God didn’t actually stop by my desk and tell me to my face that I was mediocre, but I was thinking it. And then I really did hit my knee. I had to mention that part because it’s sort of illegible.

This one is a little more realistic, and I suppose a little more optimistic:

Switching to a bottle with a narrow neck solved this particular problem. Innovation!

I can tell it’s winter in these because I drew myself in pants and a fleece jacket. I am hoping I saved the on-hold-with-IRS comic I drew from around that time, too. It unravels into annoyed madness pretty quickly.
